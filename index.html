Know More About The Author

Roger S. Peterson

Roger S. Peterson is a Professional Certified Marketer (PCM™) of the American Marketing Association, one of only 196 persons in America to gain this status. He has over 30 years of experience in sales, product management, advertising, and high-tech marketing communications (marcom) management. Unlike most marketers, Peterson had two successful sales careers and is a widely published business writer. His career includes roles as a marketing communications manager at two technology firms and as a writer for Intel’s IT department. He also managed divisions of Ogilvy & Mather and J. Walter Thompson.


Credentials and Achievements

  • Former college textbook acquisitions editor: Published 45 textbooks on psychology and teacher education.
  • Pre-publication reviewer for 24 books from Berrett-Koehler Publishers
  • Published over 200 magazine articles, including four cover stories
  • Interviewed on NBC Nightly News regarding writing
  • Past columnist for TRADESHOW Week and the Sacramento Business Journal
  • Marketing communications manager at two technology firms
  • Writer for Intel’s IT department
  • Credentialled California high school teacher
  • BA from Macalester College
